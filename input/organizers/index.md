## Nik Kantar

Nik has been a co-organizer since 2017. He is also the [PyBeach](https://pybeach.org) founder and [PyBeach 2020](https://2020.pybeach.org) chair.


## Esther Nam

Esther has been a co-organizer since 2013. She started writing Python in 2008 and has also co-founded [PyLadies](https://pyladies.org).


## Stephen Kiazyk

Stephen started organizing SoCal Python events by accident in 2025 after chairing [PyBeach 2025](https://2025.pybeach.org).


## Sarah Kuchinsky

Sarah is Tutorials Chair [PyCon US](https://us.pycon.org), helped co-found [North Bay Python](https://northbaypython.org), led [PyLadies Silicon Valley](https://www.meetup.com/pyladies-silicon-valley/) for over 5 years, is currently [PyBeach](https://pybeach.org)'s Executive Director, and is now a co-organizer for SoCal Python.


## Michael Ryabushkin
