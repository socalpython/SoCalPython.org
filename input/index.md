**SoCal Python** is a Los Angeles area Python enthusiast group.

We are currently hosting a booth at the [Southern California Linux Expo](https://www.socallinuxexpo.org/scale/23x), at the [Pasadena Convention Center](https://www.visitpasadena.com/convention-center/) (alongside [PyCon US](https://us.pycon.org/2026/) and [PyBeach](https://pybeach.org)). If you're in the neighborhood, come by and say hi!

Our next meeting will be at [O'Brien's Irish Pub](https://www.obriensla.com/) on Saturday, March 28th, at 2PM.

Join our [Meetup](https://www.meetup.com/socalpython/) or [Luma](https://luma.com/socalpython) groups, or sign up for our [Buttondown Newsletter](https://buttondown.com/socalpython) to hear about future events.
