## News

### Next Meeting
Our next meeting will be at [O'Brien's Irish Pub](https://www.obriensla.com/) on Saturday, March 28th, at 2PM.

Melinda Thielbar, PhD will be presenting a talk entitled **Practical AI: An Expert Opinion**:

<p style="margin-left: 30px;">
Everyone is talking about AI, and it's hard to tell what's real and what's just the latest headline-grabbing press release. This will be a practical guide from a technical person. We'll talk about what these models are, what they are not, how they can be used effectively, and how to prepare for what's ahead. 
</p>

<p style="margin-left: 30px;">
Melinda Thielbar is a 30-year veteran of the AI industry with a PhD in statistics. Her experience ranges from teaching programming in a dead language to optimizing power systems for navy ships to script consulting. She is currently an AI strategist for tax at a large accounting firm. This talk is based on her personal opinions and does not reflect the opinions of her employer.
</p>

Big thanks to Melinda for offering to share her time and expertise!
